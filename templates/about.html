About Us

Sri Kamalam Vidyalaya School Early Learning

Vivekananda Vidyalaya Matriculation Hr. Sec. School was started in the year 1981 as a primary school and was ungraded to a matriculation school with the great patronage from the parents.

  • Education

    Higher secondary classes have been started in the year 1998. Tamil Medium for +1 and +2 has started in 2007.

  • Location

    The school is located in a spacious and peaceful surrounding.

  • Aesthetic Campus

    The aesthetically designed campus has ultra modern facilities with spacious class rooms, separate labs for Biology, Physics, Chemistry, and Computer with Internet facility and English language lab.
